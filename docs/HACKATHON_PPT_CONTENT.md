## IDEA TITLE
### Proposed Solution (Describe your Idea/Solution/Prototype)
- **Detailed explanation of the proposed solution:**
	OMINA is a dual-mode assistance platform. In normal situations, it helps users with everyday needs (healthcare, local services, bookings, support requests). In high-risk situations, it automatically switches to emergency mode and shows critical actions, instructions, and nearest support options.
- **How it addresses the problem:**
	It solves app fragmentation by combining daily help and emergency response in one platform, reducing confusion and time-to-action during stressful moments.
- **Innovation and uniqueness of the solution:**
	The key innovation is context-adaptive UX (Daily ↔ Emergency) with an AI-based decision engine that classifies urgency and returns structured, actionable guidance.

## FEASIBILITY AND VIABILITY
- **Analysis of the feasibility of the idea:**
	Technically feasible with existing MERN infrastructure and modular services already implemented; operationally feasible through phased onboarding of local partners.
- **Potential challenges and risks:**
	AI latency, sensitive data protection, and dependency on local response provider availability.
- **Strategies for overcoming these challenges:**
	Use fallback logic and caching for reliability, enforce secure auth/encryption, and scale provider integration city-by-city.
